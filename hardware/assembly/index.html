    Assembly Guide

    This guide describes how to assemble PrintExo from 3D-printed components, off-the-shelf mechanical parts, and electronics.

    1. Overview

    PrintExo uses a modular architecture organized into three main subsystems:

    Subsystem Description
    Actuation module Motor, planetary gear stage, protective casing
    Shank structure Vertical frame, passive hinge, calf attachment
    Shoe interface Dorsal component, plantar guides, lace integration

    Assembly follows a bottom-up approach: gearbox ‚Üí actuation module ‚Üí shank ‚Üí shoe interface.

    2. Before You Begin

    Checklist

    • [ ] All 3D-printed parts completed and post-processed
    • [ ] All BOM components available (bearings, fasteners, motor)
    • [ ] Required tools prepared
    • [ ] Clean, well-lit workspace

    Recommended Preparation

    1. Verify printed parts ‚Äî check for warping, incomplete features, or layer defects
    2. Ream all holes ‚Äî use appropriate drill bits to clean up M3 holes
    3. Tap threads ‚Äî tap M3 holes where bolts will be inserted
    4. Dry-fit bearings ‚Äî ensure press-fit is snug but not cracked

    3. Required Tools

    Tool Size/Spec Purpose
    Hex key set 2.5 mm (for M3) Tightening bolts
    Screwdriver Phillips / Flat General fastening
    Drill bits 2.5 mm, 3.0 mm Hole reaming
    Hand tap M3 √ó 0.5 Threading holes
    Needle-nose pliers ‚Äî Support removal, small parts
    Calipers ‚Äî Dimensional verification
    Power drill (optional) ‚Äî Speeds up drilling/tapping

    4. Assembly Steps

    Step 1 ‚Äî Sun Gear Assembly

    Fit the Sun Gear to the Sun Gear Holder.

    Step 2 ‚Äî Align Screw Holes

    Align the screw holes.

    Step 3 ‚Äî Fasten Sun Gear

    Fasten using six (6) M3 √ó 22 mm screws until each screw contacts the bottom surface.

    Step 4 ‚Äî Mount Sun Gear to Actuation Module

    Align the Sun Gear with the six (6) screw holes on the Actuation Module (CubeMars AK60-6 V1.1 KV140). Fasten in the specified order, then tighten fully.

    ‚ö†Ô∏è Caution: Do not let any screw tilt the Sun Gear contact surface.

    Step 5 ‚Äî Planet Gear Bearing Installation

    Insert one (1) HK0810 (8√ó12√ó10) into each of the four (4) Planet Gears.

    Step 6 ‚Äî Ring Gear Installation

    Align the Ring Gear with the Actuation Module, then insert it fully.

    Step 7 ‚Äî Secure Ring Gear

    Fasten using six (6) M3 √ó 5 mm screws in the specified order, then tighten fully.

    Step 8 ‚Äî Carrier Bearing Installation

    Insert one (1) HK0810 (8√ó12√ó10) into the Carrier.

    Step 9 ‚Äî Medial Gearbox Bracket

    Align the Medial Gearbox Bracket with the bearing, then insert it fully.

    Step 10 ‚Äî Planet Gears Installation

    Align the four (4) Planet Gears with the bearings, then insert them fully.

    Step 11 ‚Äî Sun Gear Engagement

    Align the Actuation Module Sun Gear with the bearing, then insert it fully.

    ‚ö†Ô∏è Caution: Align the teeth of the four (4) Planet Gears with the teeth of the Sun Gear.

    Step 12 ‚Äî Carrier Fastening

    Insert M3 flat washers, then fasten the Carrier to the Actuation Module using two (2) M3 √ó 22 mm screws. Tighten fully.

    Step 13 ‚Äî Medial Cover

    Fasten the Medial Cover using three (3) M3 √ó 22 mm screws. Tighten fully.

    Step 14 ‚Äî Lower Bracket

    Fasten the Lower Bracket using three (3) M3 √ó 22 mm screws. Tighten fully.

    Step 15 ‚Äî Check Assembly

    Check that there are no gaps between modules.

    Step 16 ‚Äî Passive Hinge Bearings

    Insert two (2) HK0810 (8√ó12√ó10) into the holes in the Passive Hinge section of the Shank Structure.

    Step 17 ‚Äî Shank Structure & Hinge Pin

    Fit the Shank Structure to the Ring Gear. Insert the Hinge Pin, then fasten using one (1) M3 √ó 40 mm screw. Tighten fully.

    Step 18 ‚Äî Upper Bracket to Hinge Pin

    Fasten the Upper Bracket to the Hinge Pin using one (1) M3 √ó 40 mm screw. Tighten fully.

    Step 19 ‚Äî Upper Bracket to Ring Gear

    Fasten the Upper Bracket to the Ring Gear using three (3) M3 √ó 22 mm screws. Tighten fully.

    Step 20 ‚Äî Calf Compression Sleeve & Strap Anchor

    Fasten the Calf Compression Sleeve and Strap Anchor to the Shank Structure using four (4) M3 √ó 22 mm screws. Tighten fully.

    ‚ö†Ô∏è Caution: Select the size option based on the user's shank length, then fasten through the corresponding holes.

    Step 21 ‚Äî Wire Guide

    Fasten the Wire Guide using four (4) M3 √ó 22 mm screws. Tighten fully.

    Step 22 ‚Äî Dorsal Interface

    Fasten the shoe-mounted Dorsal Interface to the Carrier using four (4) M3 √ó 20 mm screws. Tighten fully.

    ‚ÑπÔ∏è Note:
    This step attaches the dorsal interface to the device.
    Instructions for mounting the dorsal interface onto the shoe are provided in the
    Shoelace Fixation Guide.

    Step 23 ‚Äî Auxiliary Lace

    Use the Auxiliary Lace to secure the Dorsal Interface and Carrier assembly to the shoe.

    ‚ÑπÔ∏è Note:
    Detailed lace routing and tying instructions are provided in the
    Shoelace Fixation Guide.

    Step 24 ‚Äî Complete üéâ

    Assembly complete.

    5. Alignment & Fit Verification

    After assembly, perform the following checks:

    Mechanical Checks

    Check Expected Result
    Gearbox rotation Smooth, no grinding or binding
    Hinge movement Free inversion/eversion motion
    Motor operation Spins without obstruction
    Shoe interface rigidity No wobble at mounting point

    Fit Checks

    • [ ] Shank aligned with lateral side of lower leg
    • [ ] Ankle joint axis approximately aligned with device axis
    • [ ] Calf sleeve secure while comfortable
    • [ ] Laces tensioned evenly across dorsal interface
    • [ ] No pinch points during range of motion test

    6. Safety Notes

    • Do not over-tighten M3 bolts ‚Äî PLA can crack under excessive torque
    • Inspect all structural joints before human testing
    • Check for pinch points around hinge and gearbox
